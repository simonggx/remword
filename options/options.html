Help & Support

Learn how to use RemWord effectively

Getting Started

  1. Select any text on a webpage to see instant translation
  2. Click "Save to Vocabulary" to add words to your collection
  3. Use the Practice section to reinforce your learning
  4. Track your progress on the Dashboard

Features

  • Text Selection: Highlight text to get instant translations
  • Vocabulary Storage: Save words with context and source
  • Practice Exercises: Multiple exercise types to reinforce learning
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor your learning journey
  • Data Export/Import: Backup and restore your vocabulary

Tips for Effective Learning

  • Practice regularly - consistency is key to vocabulary retention
  • Review words in context to understand usage better
  • Set realistic daily goals and stick to them
  • Use the mastery system to focus on difficult words

Troubleshooting

  • Translation not working: Check your internet connection
  • Words not saving: Ensure the extension has proper permissions
  • Practice not loading: Try refreshing the page
